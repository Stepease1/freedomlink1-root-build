# Oversight, Appeals, and Sanctions Framework

**Established**: 2026-07-02  
**Authority**: Governance Council

## I. Oversight Mechanisms

### 1.1 Continuous Monitoring
- All governance actions are logged
- Cryptographic verification of all decisions
- Regular audit by independent auditors
- Community can request verification

### 1.2 Quarterly Reviews
- Council conducts governance review
- Compliance with charter verified
- Procedures checked against standards
- Report published to community

### 1.3 Transparency Requirements
- All decisions documented publicly
- Rationale for actions explained
- Dissenting opinions preserved
- Record of votes maintained

## II. Appeals Process

### 2.1 Grounds for Appeal
Community members may appeal:
- Governance decisions
- Permission denials
- Amendment rejections
- Sanction decisions
- Eligibility determinations

### 2.2 Appeal Filing
1. Written appeal submitted within 30 days
2. Includes:
   - Description of decision being appealed
   - Grounds for appeal
   - Requested remedy
   - Supporting evidence
3. Filed with Council

### 2.3 Appeal Review
**Timeline**: 14 days review, 7 days decision

1. Appeals Committee formed (3 independent members)
2. Original decision reviewed
3. Appellee given opportunity to respond
4. Hearing held (if requested)
5. Decision rendered with written justification

### 2.4 Appeal Outcomes
- **Upheld**: Original decision stands
- **Reversed**: Decision overturned, remedy applied
- **Remanded**: Returned for reconsideration
- **Partially Modified**: Decision adjusted

### 2.5 Appeal Rights
- All parties have equal access
- Impartial arbiters required
- Written decision always provided
- Further appeal to Root Authority (final)

## III. Sanctions Framework

### 3.1 Sanction Levels

**Level 1: Warning**
- Documented for first violation
- No rights restrictions
- Educational in nature
- Expires after 6 months

**Level 2: Suspension**
- Temporary loss of voting rights
- Duration: 30-90 days
- Can be appealed
- Record preserved

**Level 3: Probation**
- Extended monitoring period
- 90-180 day duration
- Restricted permissions
- Regular compliance reviews

**Level 4: Removal**
- Permanent loss of contributor status
- Can appeal
- Records preserved for history
- Option to reapply after 1 year

### 3.2 Sanctionable Violations
- Charter violations
- Unauthorized modifications
- Breach of cryptographic procedures
- Repeated policy violations
- Breach of confidentiality
- Sabotage of system integrity

### 3.3 Sanction Procedures
1. Violation documented with evidence
2. Notice given to member (7 days to respond)
3. Investigation if contested
4. Sanction hearing held
5. Decision made by Council majority
6. Member may appeal
7. Decision recorded and time-stamped

### 3.4 Proportionality
- Sanctions must be proportionate to violation
- Severity considered in determination
- Mitigating factors reviewed
- Intent distinguished from accident

## IV. Dispute Resolution

### 4.1 Informal Resolution
1. Direct negotiation between parties
2. Mediation by neutral party
3. Documentation of resolution
4. Agreement recorded

### 4.2 Formal Arbitration
1. Request filed with Arbitration Board
2. Arbiters selected (3 neutral parties)
3. Evidence presented by both sides
4. Hearing conducted
5. Decision rendered within 30 days
6. Decision is binding

### 4.3 Escalation
- Appeals from arbitration to Root Authority
- Root Authority decision is final
- Documented for future reference

## V. Record Keeping

All oversight, appeals, and sanctions are:
- Recorded with dates and signatures
- Preserved in permanent registry
- Blockchain anchored
- Cryptographically verified
- Accessible to community for audit

## VI. Restoration

### 6.1 Conditions for Restoration
Members may request restoration after:
- Probation period completion
- Demonstrated compliance
- Request filed with Council
- Council votes on restoration

### 6.2 Restoration Process
1. Member submits application
2. Council reviews compliance history
3. Community has 14 days to comment
4. Council votes (supermajority required)
5. Decision recorded and communicated

## VII. Authority Structure

**Oversight Committee**: Monitors governance  
**Appeals Committee**: Hears appeals (3 members)  
**Arbitration Board**: Resolves disputes (3-5 members)  
**Root Authority**: Final arbiter and appeals reviewer  

## VIII. Reporting

Annual report published including:
- Sanctions imposed
- Appeals filed and outcomes
- Disputes resolved
- Trends and improvements
- Recommendations for framework updates
